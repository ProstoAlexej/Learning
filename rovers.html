PrOP-M

PrOP-M were the first rovers to be launched to Mars, 26 years before the first successful rover mission of NASA's Sojourner in 1997.

Sojourner

Sojourner is a robotic Mars rover that landed in the Ares Vallis channel in the Chryse Planitia region of the Oxia Palus quadrangle on July 4, 1997.

Wikipedia
Spirit

Spirit, also known as MER-A (Mars Exploration Rover – A) or MER-2, is a Mars robotic rover, active from 2004 to 2010.Spirit was operational on Mars for 2208 sols or 3.3 Martian years.

Wikipedia
Opportunity

Opportunity, also known as MER-B (Mars Exploration Rover – B) or MER-1, and nicknamed Oppy, was a robotic rover that was active on Mars from 2004 until mid-2018. Opportunity was operational on Mars for 5110 sols.

Wikipedia
Curiosity

Curiosity is a car-sized Mars rover designed to explore the Gale crater on Mars as part of NASA's Mars Science Laboratory (MSL) mission.

Wikipedia
Perseverance

Perseverance, nicknamed Percy,[2] is a car-sized Mars rover designed to explore the Jezero crater on Mars as part of NASA's Mars 2020 mission.

Wikipedia
Zhurong

Zhurong (Chinese: 祝融; pinyin: Zhùróng) is an active Mars rover that is China's first rover to land on another planet (having previously landed two rovers on the Moon).
